Integrate Coastal Resilience Hub

The Team
Award receiving moment
Award receiving moment

Recipient: Gopalganj Science and Technology University (GSTU) Science Club.
Project Title: Integrated Coastal Resilience Hub: Citizen Science for Climate Adaptation and Pollution Change Management.
Funding & Grant: Awarded a 3 Lakh BDT research grant from the "Youth Innovation Fund 2025: Sponsoring Green Idea" competition, organized by ICCCAD and funded by the Government of Sweden.
Highlights: A multidisciplinary team of 5 student researchers, supervised by Dr. Neaz Al Hasan, embarked on a two-day field trip to the Sundarbans (Dublar Char and Hiron Point) to collect critical environmental data and lead citizen science initiatives.
