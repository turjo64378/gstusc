Our Specialized Departments

The club splits into targeted operational teams so members can deep-dive into what they enjoy most.

🤖 Robotics & Hardware

Deals directly with microcontrollers, autonomous systems, circuit prototyping, drone mechanics, and engineering design contests.

💻 Software & IT

Focuses on web deployment, script development, algorithmic problem solving, cybersecurity tasks, and fast-paced hackathons.

🌌 Astronomy

Dedicated entirely to observation. Manages deep-sky telescope rigs, field stargazing operations, and astrophysics theoretical studies.

🧬 Life Sciences

Explores chemistry experiments, structural molecular setups, bio-tech alignments, and collaborative environmental clean-tech designs.
